Course Highlights

4 Days · Classroom + Intensive Hands-On Labs

Developed As The "Next Level" Of Training, The Program Teaches Advanced Knowledge And Skills To Students Who Already Have Introductory Training Or Several Years Of Field Experience. The Curriculum Is Delivered By Light Brigade Certified Trainers And Aligned To The Workflows Used Across Hyperscale And FTTx Deployments.

Six Core Modules

Introduction

  • Basic Fiber Optic Terminology
  • FTTx Optical Fiber Transmission Systems
  • Typical Transmission Rates
  • Why Use Fiber Optics?
  • Fiber Optic Standards

FTTx PON Methodology

  • Passive Optical Networks (PON)
  • FTTH, FTTB, POLAN, FTTC
  • Active Ethernet, TDM / TDMA
  • B-PON, G-PON, 10G-PON, EPON
  • RF Over Glass, WDM-PON

Topologies

  • Point-To-Point & Star
  • Reach Extender, Route Redundancy
  • Ring, Mesh And Bus Topologies

  • Cable & Fiber Management
