Modular Fiber Management

High-Density Cassette Systems For Scalable Deployments

AFL's DENALI™ And U-Series Cassette Platforms Bring Fast, Repeatable Fiber Termination To Hyperscale And Enterprise Deployments — From Field Splicing To Pre-Terminated, Plug-And-Play Connectivity.

DENALI™ Splice Cassettes

Compact, High-Density Splice Trays Built For Fast Field Termination. Supports Both Single-Fiber And Ribbon Splicing With Factory Pre-Routed Pigtails, Keeping Installation Quick And Insertion Loss Low.

DENALI™ MPO Fanout Cassettes

Configurable From Base-8 Through Base-24, With Up To 24 Fibers Per Cassette For True Plug-And-Play Connectivity. Shuttered Adapters And Polarity F Mapping Keep Large-Scale Rollouts Fast And Error-Free.

U-Series MPO Fanout Cassettes

Pre-Terminated MPO-To-LC Fanout Cassettes That Turn Backbone Trunks Into Ready-To-Patch LC Ports — Speeding Up Deployments And Simplifying Future Network Expansions.
